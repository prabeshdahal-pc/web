About the Event

The Nepal National Tech Summit 2024 is a premier gathering of innovators, entrepreneurs, and thought leaders aimed at igniting discussions around technology and its impact on society. This event seeks to inspire action, foster new connections, and highlight the power of technology in transforming lives.

Our mission is to provide a platform for speakers from diverse backgrounds to share insights and experiences that will accelerate intellectual growth and collaboration across various fields, including entrepreneurship, innovation, and women empowerment. Join us to explore new frontiers in tech and network with leading figures in the industry.

About the Nepal National Tech Summit


The Nepal National Tech Summit 2024 is designed to bring together innovators, leaders, and visionaries from the tech community to foster collaboration and ignite ideas. This summit will provide a unique platform for participants to engage in inspiring talks, insightful discussions, and networking opportunities. With a focus on entrepreneurship, digital transformation, and technology's role in sustainable development, the summit aims to empower individuals and organizations to drive impactful change in Nepal's tech landscape.
